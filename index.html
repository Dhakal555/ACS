About our charity

ACS is the small charity shows and the non profitable organization that focuses on the welfare of the animals that are in need, animals that needs extra support. Our organization conducts annual charity show each year to raise enough fund for the animals that are in need. Our team mates have been assigned to different animal shelters around the country and to manage the animals and their shelter the organization is involved in the charity activity annually.
